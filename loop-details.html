Loop

  • Category: Mobile dating app
  • Built with: Dart, Flutter, Firebase
  • Article URL: https://www.bowdoin.edu/news/2019/05/a-romantic-campus-tradition-gets-an-upgrade
  • App listing URL: https://appadvice.com/app/loop-dating-app

I built this with three friends as a class project senior year of college. Two of us worked on design & marketing, while another peer and I co-coded the app.

Although no longer available on the App Store or Google Play Store because we didn't keep up with listing fees, Loop was downloaded by 300+ users the month it was launched.
